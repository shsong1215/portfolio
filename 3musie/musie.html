PROJECT BACKGROUND

Musie

At HackHarvard 2016, we had to build a technology using Microsoft Cognitive API. Our team, Hyo Bin Yang, Sun Min Lim, Christine Song, Daniel Chan, and Yucun Li, built a front and back-end of Musie — a music player that uses Microsoft’s Emotion API and Spotify mood playlists. The inspiration for this application stemmed from the inconvenience of searching for the right music at the right time. Why not use your facial expressions (or others) and let us handle the songs for you!
