PROJECT BACKGROUND

Talent Campaign

Talent is a campaign to promote that talent donation is for everyone. Talent donation encourages people to help certain community in any kind of ways each year.

Donation is not hard for anyone if it is in any kinds of form. It creates healthy community and better world. Talent campaign target certain community each year and share any kinds of talent. It can be drawing, singing, cooking and even performing!

Former communities we helped include:
Homeless in Providence, Society for the Blind, Young Immigrant laborers and Comfort Women in North Korea.
