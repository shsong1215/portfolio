PROJECT BACKGROUND

Bubbles

“Bubbles” is a project driven from a personal interest of relationship between web and print designs. People differentiate print design and web design as totally separate things these days. Often in interviews, they ask me whether I am a print based designer or web based designer.

I understand that there are several sides of design that designers can specialize into. However, I believe that everyone needs a basic understanding that design lives on the same platform to begin with.
